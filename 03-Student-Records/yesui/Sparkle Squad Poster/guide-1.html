Mission Briefing & Team Roles

15 min
✨ What I do
  • Gather my crew and announce: “Today I'm the Director!”
  • Draw my plan poster — all 7 stations in order.
  • Hand out a job (and a smile) to everyone.
👫 Crew jobs

Big Bro (12): Co-Captain — keeps us on time & reads the plan.

Little Bro (3): picks our team color & a cheer.

🧺 I'll need

Big paper, markers, stickers, my Sparkle Squad poster.

🍓
12:45
2

Sparkle Chef Kitchen

60 min
✨ What I do
  • Wash hands & put on our aprons.
  • Build rainbow fruit & yogurt cups, sprinkle “sparkle” on top.
  • Count cups so everyone gets one — then we eat together!
👫 Crew jobs

Big Bro: helps slice fruit (grown-up watching).

Little Bro: sprinkles toppings & taste-tests. 😋

🧺 I'll need

Fruit, yogurt, cups, spoons, sprinkles, an adult helper.

💎
1:45
3

Glitter Friendship Bracelets

60 min
✨ What I do
  • Plan a color pattern — pink, silver, glitter… repeat!
  • String a bracelet for Mom, Dad, Big Bro & Little Bro.
  • Add a sparkly name tag to each one.
👫 Crew jobs

Big Bro: makes his own bracelet beside me.

Little Bro: threads chunky beads with my help (no tiny pieces!).

🧺 I'll need

Elastic string, big & small beads, glitter glue, name tags, scissors.

🛠️
2:45
4

Build the Sparkle Sports Course

45 min
✨ What I do
  • Draw a map of 3 zones: gymnastics, soccer, running.
  • Set up cones, a balance line & a goal.
  • Write the game rules on a card.
👫 Crew jobs

Big Bro: building partner — carries gear & tests each zone.

Little Bro: gets a mini “toddler zone” (soft toss + waddle race).

🧺 I'll need

Cones/cups, soccer ball, a mat or towel, tape, my course map.
