## 🎬 REEL 1 — "You're smart. Just in the wrong place."

**On-screen hook (0–3 sec):**
> **"You're smart. Just in the wrong place."**

**Narration / caption:**
Ken Robinson said something that should make you uncomfortable.
Most people spend their entire lives doing work that was never meant for them.
Not because they're lazy. Not because they're not smart enough.
Because they never asked two questions *at the same time:*
"What am I naturally good at?"
"What makes me completely lose track of time?"
