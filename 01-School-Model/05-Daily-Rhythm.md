# 5\. The Daily Rhythm {#5.-the-daily-rhythm}

No rigid schedule. A predictable, purposeful structure every day.

5.1 Why structure exists — to create conditions for growth, not compliance

5.2 The key moments every student moves through daily:

- **Start of Day** — physical and mental readiness: unorganised fun, movement, mood, mindset
- **Daily Results Setting** — student commits to what they will accomplish today
- **Deep Work** — sustained project work, inquiry, building
- **Knowledge Acquisition** — AI Tutor sessions, just-in-time learning
- **Nourishment Hour** — students cook and eat real food together
- **End of Day** — peer feedback exchange, daily reflection
