# 6\. Who Supports the Student {#6.-who-supports-the-student}

Human relationships are the centre. AI is the infrastructure.

6.1 The Guide

* Four-part teaching framework: Inspire · Enable · Expect · Embed

* Key responsibilities: design, scaffold, coach, assess formatively, connect community

* What a Guide does that AI never can: ignite passion, cultivate Element, provide genuine empathy, model vulnerability

6.2 The Socratic AI Tutor

* Four operational phases: Hook · Mastery · Guardrail · Synthesis

* The hard boundary: AI is a cognitive scaffold. The Guide is the architect of the student's Element.

6.3 Peers

* Mixed-age project teams

* Critique and revision as daily practice, not a grade event

6.4 Mentors and Community Experts

* Real-world practitioners connected to the student's project domain

* Public audiences for final products

6.5 Family

* Active partners in the student's learning — not passive recipients of report cards
