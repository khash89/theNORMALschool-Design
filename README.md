# theNORMALschool — Design & Development

*Designed for Life.*

This repository holds the design, pedagogical model, research foundations, student
records, and outreach materials for **theNORMALschool**. Folders are numbered to give
a logical reading order: **design → evidence → application → outreach.**

## Map

| Folder | What lives here |

| [`01-School-Model/`](01-School-Model/) | The full school design, one file per section (Parts 1–3 + Projects, Daily Rhythm, Support, Assessment, Graduation), plus deep-dive references for the Four Domains and the Socratic AI Tutor. Start at [`_index.md`](01-School-Model/_index.md). |

## Conventions

- **Filenames** use `Kebab-Case.md` (no spaces, em-dashes, or `Snake_Case`). Code/asset files (`index.html`, `server.js`, fonts, images) keep their conventional lowercase names.
- **Folders** use kebab-case or `Title-Case`, never spaces (e.g. `Sparkle-Squad-Poster/`, not `Sparkle Squad Poster/`).
- **Student documents** are versioned in the filename (`Student-Hypothesis-v2.1.md`) and
  live in a per-student folder (`03-Student-Records/<Student-Name>/`).
- **Document versions** are tracked in a Revision Log table at the bottom of each living
  document, not in separate files.

## Core concepts (quick reference)

- **The Element** — the intersection of a student's natural aptitude and what genuinely
  compels them (after Ken Robinson). The foundation the rest of the model builds on.
- **The Four Domains of Development** — what students develop: the Element, Knowledge,
  Timeless Competencies, and Whole Child. See [`01-School-Model/09-Appendix-Four-Domains.md`](01-School-Model/09-Appendix-Four-Domains.md).
- **Student Hypothesis** — a working, evidence-based judgment about how a student grows.
  Not a label. Revised every cycle.
- **Personal Pathway** — the active per-cycle plan that translates the Hypothesis into
  projects, knowledge targets, competencies, and support.
